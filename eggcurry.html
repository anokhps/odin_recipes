Egg Curry

onion and tomatoes form the base of egg curry and you can use the in one of the three forms.Fine chopped,grated or pureed.The flavour and taste of the egg curry depends on how make them together.

The onion and tomotoe puree gives the best results as the dish not use fatoil anything like that,and also its not using cream,nuts and any other industrialized flavour to the dish.

Ingredients

  • Tomatoe
  • Onion
  • Two Eggs
  • Cocunut Oil

Instruction

  1. Chop tomatoes and oinion slightly
  2. Mix the copped tomatoe and onion with the eggy
  3. Put cocunut oil on the pan and heat it in low flame
  4. Put the mix of mixed onion,tomatoe and the eggy into the pan and cook five minutes
