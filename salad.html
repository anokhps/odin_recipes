Salad

Salad recipes are my favorite way to showcase vibrant, in-season produce fruits and veggies that are so good on their own that you dont need to do much to make them into a delicious meal. On cold winter nights, I like to brighten up our dinner table with a big, colorful mix of root veggies and hearty greens. On beautiful, warm days when I would rather be outside than in the kitchen, summer salads are the perfect solution: they are simple to toss together, but they are totally delicious nonetheless.

Ingredients

  • Tomatoe
  • Cucumber
  • Carrot
  • Olive Oil

Instruction

  1. Slice tomatoe,cucumber and carrot
  2. Add some water
  3. Mix them with olive oil
